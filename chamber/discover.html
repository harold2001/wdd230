Mission

The Lima Chamber of Commerce promotes the general commercial interests of local companies and businesses. We promote our members' trade through statistics, advertising, training, lunches and market networking.

Vision

To be the leading and reference entity in promoting trade, investment, and business innovation in Lima and nationwide.

Services and Benefits

Business Advisory

  • Legal Consulting: Offering guidance on legal matters including corporate law, intellectual property, and labor law.
  • Accounting Services: Providing support in financial planning, tax advisory, and auditing.
  • Workshops and Seminars: Regularly held training sessions to keep business owners and entrepreneurs updated on best practices and emerging trends.

Networking

  • Networking Events: Organizing events such as business breakfasts, industry roundtables, and networking nights to help members build valuable connections.
  • International Connections: Providing access to a broad network of national and international contacts to explore new business opportunities.
  • Business Roundtables: Facilitating discussions and partnerships among members from various sectors.

Highlighted Events

  • Lima Business Expo: The largest trade fair in the region, where companies can exhibit their products and services to a broad audience. The expo includes workshops, product demonstrations, and networking sessions.
  • International Trade Seminar: Conferences and workshops that cover best practices and opportunities in international trade. Topics include export-import strategies, global market trends, and trade compliance.

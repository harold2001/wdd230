Lima Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Purpose

The Lima Chamber of Commerce is a powerful tool that allows its members to participate in local businesses and other organizations. We boost the productivity of various sectors and provide informational, statistical and economic support.

The Lima Chamber of Commerce promotes the general commercial interests of local companies and businesses. We promote our members' trade through statistics, advertising, training, lunches and market networking.

Scenarios

What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?

This month, the chamber will host a series of business breakfasts comprised of a weekly networking breakfast that brings together entrepreneurs and business leaders to discuss industry trends, share ideas and explore opportunities for collaboration in an informal setting. We will also hold the Lima Business Expo, a large-scale exhibition where local companies can showcase their products and services, facilitating direct connections with potential partners and clients.

What has been the population growth in the area?

The population growth in the Lima area has seen a steady increase over the past decade. According to recent census data and local demographic reports, Lima's population has grown by approximately 1.5% to 2% annually. This growth can be attributed to several factors, including economic development, improved infrastructure, and the city's appeal as a cultural and business hub.
